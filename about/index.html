What we do

We help you turn messages into sales. Here’s how: Bring all your messages (WhatsApp, Instagram, emails, calls) into one place. Sort out the noise so you see only what matters. Why we do what we do

Our flagship voice bot service is designed to work around the clock, seamlessly managing tasks like handling customer queries, booking appointments, following up, and collecting valuable feedback. By automating these essential interactions, Sonic Linker allows business owners to focus on what they do best while we handle the details that keep customers coming back.
